## Triage notes

- `Accepted` means the direction is approved, but implementation still requires a bounded change and explicit execution authorization.
- `Converted` means at least one linked change exists; it does not imply the recommendation is fully delivered.
- One recommendation may link to multiple changes, and one change may link to multiple recommendations.
- Keep rejected and deferred items visible unless they are spam or exact duplicates.
